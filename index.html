Rules

Choose betwen 3 categories: Animals, music bands/singers and instruments.

Guess the letters in the secret word to solve the puzzle.

You can guess a letter by clicking it.

If that letter is in the word it will appear in every place it belongs.

If the letter isn't in the word then a body part will be added to the gallows.

You can keep guessing letters until the word is solved or until all six body parts are on the gallows.

Good luck and have fun!
